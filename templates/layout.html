Tips on searching:

It helps to do Topic - Trait + Trait. This helps 'replace' the parts you subtract with a better match insetad of removing it entirely.

Common, single-word topics with little ambiguity work best. I've replaced many words with their canonical forms with help from Freebase and Wikipedia, but ambiguity always lurks.

The Matrix - Thoughtful + Dumb
Harry Truman - American + Russian
Old Navy, Banana Republic, Macys, Louis Vuitton
Mitt Romney - Experience + Celebrity
Darth Vader - Cape
Marriage - Sex
